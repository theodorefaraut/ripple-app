Getting started

Why authorize Apple Music?

Ripple needs access to your library to display your playlists and let you add tracks.

Subscription required?

Yes, an active Apple Music subscription is required to use Ripple.

Modes

The two modes

Favorites: swipe right adds to favorites.
Playlist: swipe right adds to a playlist of your choice.

Change mode

Tap the hand icon at the top of the screen. Choose Favorites or Playlist, then select the target playlist.

Common questions

What is "For You"?

Playlists recommended by Apple Music based on your taste. Great for discovering new tracks.

Grayed out tracks?

These are tracks you've already processed. They remain visible and interactive.

New playlist not showing?

Recently created playlists may take a few minutes to appear. Pull down to refresh.

Add to a specific playlist?

From the track list, tap + next to the heart. Choose a playlist or create a new one.

Trust

Does Ripple delete my tracks?

No, never. Ripple never deletes any tracks. It helps you decide faster.

Delete my data?

All data is local. Delete the app to erase everything.

Is the app free?

Yes. Ripple is free and ad-free.
