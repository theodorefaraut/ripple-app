Getting started

Apple Music authorization

Ripple requires access to your Apple Music library to display your playlists and tracks.

Subscription required

An active Apple Music subscription is required to use Ripple.

Modes

Change mode

Tap the hand icon at the top. Choose the mode and target playlist.

Common questions

For You

Playlists recommended by Apple Music based on your taste.

Grayed tracks

Already processed tracks. They remain visible and interactive.

Playlist not showing

New playlists may take a few minutes to appear. Pull to refresh.

Add to playlist

From the list, tap + next to the heart to choose a playlist.

Trust

Track deletion?

No. Ripple never deletes your tracks. It helps you decide.

Delete my data

All data is local. Delete the app to erase everything.

Free?

Yes. Ripple is free and ad-free.
