Ripple

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Summary

Ripple does not collect any personal data.

No analytics. No tracking. No external servers. Your data stays on your device.

Data accessed

The app only accesses:

  • Your Apple Music library
  • Your local preferences
Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Display your playlists
  • Add tracks
  • Create playlists
Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Only Apple Music API calls are made to access your library.

Third parties

Ripple uses the Apple MusicKit API. Use is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Revoke access from Settings > Privacy > Media & Apple Music
  • Delete the app and all local data
