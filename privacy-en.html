Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

Ripple ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data collected

Ripple does not collect any personal data.

The app only accesses:

  • Your Apple Music library (playlists, tracks)
  • Your sorting preferences (stored locally on your device)

Data usage

Your Apple Music library data is used only to:

  • Display your playlists in the app
  • Allow you to sort your tracks
  • Add tracks to your Apple Music favorites or playlists

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers, except for calls to the Apple Music API to access your library.

Data sharing

Ripple does not share any data with third parties.

Third-party services

The app uses the Apple MusicKit API to access your library. Use of this service is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Revoke Ripple's access to your Apple Music library from your device settings
  • Delete the app and all associated local data

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, contact us: theodore.faraut@gmail.com

Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.
